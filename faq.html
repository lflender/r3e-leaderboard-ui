Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to questions you may have about the RaceRoom leaderboards database and how to use this site.

I noticed the "Support r3e-leaderboards" button in the footer. Why would I want to pay for this site?

Servers and domain have a cost each month.

Donations allow to cover the costs of the infrastructure.


Also, developping this site takes time and effort, so you can donate as a reward for the hard work.
Each donation means a lot to me.

I just set a new record, why does it not appear right away?

All data served by RaceRoom Leaderboards Explorer is pre-fetched and does not hit the official servers.


By design, the information displayed is not live data.

So, when will my new record appear?

All data is refreshed daily, except the daily races combinations which are refreshed hourly.


Updates take a long time, but during that time, data is indexed every 30 minutes.
You can see update details in the "Database Status" section at the bottom of each page.


Worst case scenario is that you have to wait for 24 hours.

In the class filters, why are there some Categories at start?

Categories are used to show similar classes together.


The categories are based on the official Performance Index.


On the Track Info page, you can even hit the "Combine" checkbox to group multiple classes on the same leaderboards.

On the combined leaderboards, why are there some Combined car entries?

The Combined entries allow to filter the leaderboards by grouping multiple cars together when those cars have the exact same specifications.


For example, the Ferrari 296 GT3 and the Ferrari 296 GT3 DTM are the same cars, so you can filter leaderboards with both at the same time.
